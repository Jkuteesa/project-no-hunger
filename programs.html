Our Programs

School Feeding Program

We provide daily nutritious meals to over 10,000 children in 50 schools across Uganda's most food-insecure regions. This program ensures children have the energy to learn and grow, reducing school dropout rates and improving academic performance.

Climate-Smart Agriculture Training

We train smallholder farmers in climate-smart agriculture techniques, focusing on drought-resistant crops, water conservation, and soil management. This program has helped 2,000 farmers increase their crop yields by an average of 30%.

Community Gardens Initiative

We've established 100 community gardens in urban and rural areas, providing fresh produce to over 5,000 families. These gardens also serve as training grounds for sustainable farming practices and nutrition education.

Nutrition Education and Cooking Classes

Our nutrition experts conduct workshops on balanced diets and proper food preparation, reaching over 15,000 individuals annually. These classes focus on utilizing locally available, nutritious foods to combat malnutrition.
