About No Hunger Uganda

Our Mission

No Hunger Uganda is dedicated to eradicating hunger and malnutrition in Uganda through sustainable agriculture, education, and community empowerment. We believe that access to nutritious food is a fundamental human right and essential for Uganda's development.

The Hunger Crisis in Uganda

Uganda faces significant challenges in food security:

  • 19.1% of Ugandan households are food insecure
  • 29% of children under 5 suffer from chronic malnutrition
  • Climate change is affecting crop yields and food production
  • Limited access to modern farming techniques and technologies

Our Approach

We tackle hunger through a multi-faceted approach:

  1. Immediate food relief for vulnerable communities
  2. Sustainable agriculture training for smallholder farmers
  3. Establishment of community gardens and food banks
  4. Nutrition education and cooking classes
  5. Advocacy for policies that promote food security
