No Hunger Uganda

Empowering communities to overcome food insecurity

The Challenge We Face

In Uganda, over 19% of households are food insecure, with nearly 41% of people unable to afford a healthy diet. Chronic malnutrition affects 29% of children under 5, impacting their growth and future potential.

Our Impact

Since 2020, No Hunger Uganda has:

  • Provided over 500,000 nutritious meals to vulnerable communities
  • Established 100 community gardens in food-insecure regions
  • Trained 2,000 smallholder farmers in climate-smart agriculture techniques
